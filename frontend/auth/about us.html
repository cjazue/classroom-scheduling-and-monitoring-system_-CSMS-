ABOUT US

We are the students of BSIT 1-10 from the College of Engineering and Information Technology at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Valenzuela. This initiative represents our collective effort to merge technical innovation with the practical needs of our university community.

OUR VISION

At the heart of this project is a commitment to optimizing the educational environment at PLV. We recognize that as our institution grows, the traditional, manual methods of managing classroom spaces are limited and inefficient. Our booking and scheduling system, built using a digital platform, is our step forward—a transformative solution designed to grant access for everyone on campus.

What We Offer

The Web-Based Classroom Occupancy Monitoring and Scheduling System was designed to be more than just a digital logbook. It is a centralized hub that empowers our community with:

  • Instant Visibility: Users can access real-time updates on room availability, helping students and faculty find suitable spaces without the need for physical inquiries.
  • Intelligent Coordination: By implementing automated conflict detection, our system eliminates the risk of double-bookings and overlapping reservations.
  • Secure Accessibility: With a role-based authentication system, we ensure that data remains accurate and that administrative controls are handled securely by authorized personnel.
  • Adaptive Design: The platform is optimized for use across various devices, including smartphones and tablets, ensuring information is available whenever and wherever it is needed.

HOW TO USE OUR SYSTEM

    1. Start

    User opens the system and views the Welcome/Login page.

    2. Login Process

    User enters credentials and successfully logs in to access the system.

    3. Dashboard Display

    System shows the dashboard with user reservations and options.

    4. Select Campus

    User clicks "Campus" and chooses the desired campus for reservation.

    5. Select Building & Room

    User selects a building, then chooses an available room and schedule.

    6. Autofill & Confirm Reservation

    System displays the autofill form user reviews and submits the reservation.

    7. Update Dashboard

    System saves the reservation and reflects it on the dashboard as pending/confirmed.
